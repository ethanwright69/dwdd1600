About Us

Goldfish Hypermarket began as a simple lionchu breeding farm in Shenzhen, China in 2012. Since then, we have expanded our services throughout the country with 7 physical market locations, and to you - right here on the web! To this day we still pride ourselves on our charming lionchu goldfish, but also have a broad selection of:

  • Ranchu
  • Orandas

Within the past couple years we have also began to include a limited inventory of:

  • Ryukin
  • Bubble eyes
  • Telescopes

In addition to our fish selection, we also sell premium goldfish food so you can keep your pets in good health.

Growth-promoting
Nutritious protien blend including squid liver power and krill to help your fish grow properly
Anti-bloating
Whitefish-based protien powder that alleviates and prevents digestive system gas buildup
Health-fortifying
Our special blend of pellets made of shrimp and dehydrated mikan oranges to keep your fish swimming strong

We look forward to providing for all your goldfish needs! Feel free to tag us in your fish pictures on instagram @goldfishhypermarket, or post your fish to the goldfish subreddit!
